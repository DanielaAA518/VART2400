SEASON 4

Black Mirror proves with its fourth season that the series still has ample source material to terrify fans with technology that is now -- or soon could become -- an integral part of our lives.

USS Callister

By day, Robert Daly is the CTO of a tech-entertainment company, but by night he is in his own private cinematic universe -- based on a TV show he watched as a child -- as the commander of a starship exploring new worlds.

Arkangel

Worried about her daughter's safety, Marie signs up for a cutting-edge device that will monitor the girl's location.

Crocodile

Mia scrambles to keep a dark secret; Shazia harvests people's memories of an accident scene.

Hang the DJ

Paired up by a dating program that puts an end date on relationships, Frank and Amy begin to question the system's logic.

Metalhead

While searching for supplies at an abandoned warehouse, scavengers encounter a ruthless foe and flee for their lives.

Black Museum

A traveler comes across a museum that boasts rare criminal artifacts and a disturbing main attraction.
