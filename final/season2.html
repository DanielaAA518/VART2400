SEASON 2

Another compact season of Black Mirror delivers chilling, insightful, and even emotionally wrought stories about technology that stay with the audience long after viewing.

Be Right Back

Young couple Martha and Ash move into a remote cottage; Ash is killed the very next day; at his funeral, Martha's friend Sarah tells her about a new service that lets people stay in touch with the deceased.

White Bear

TA young woman, Victoria, wakes up and cannot remember anything about her life; everyone she encounters refuses to communicate with her and enjoys filming her discomfort on their phones.

The Waldo Moment

The production company behind a satirical late-night comedy show develops a pilot for one of its characters, a blue CGI bear named Waldo; they enter Waldo in a political race to generate publicity for the new series; the scheme gets out of control.

White Christmas

In a mysterious and remote snowy outpost, Matt and Potter share an interesting Christmas meal together, swapping creepy tales of their earlier lives in the outside world.
